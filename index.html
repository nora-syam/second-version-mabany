Greener Side of Life

According to “Green 5” success and high demand on as is one of the most popular residential compounds that attracted many people due to its genius designs, distinguished location, vast green area, parks and gardens, the owner company decided to persist in the success story and develop “Green 6”.

Location

The compound is located in the Northern Expansions area in the 6th of October City beside “Green 5” compound. It is close to the main axes such as the Rawd Al-Farag Corridor and several other vital areas, such as Mall of Arabia (5 minutes away), Juhayna Square (5 minutes away), Shooting Club (3 minutes away), Tivoli Dome Mall, The 6th of October University, Nile University, and University of Egypt.

facilities

Residents are guaranteed privacy and support that allows them to feel more relaxed and enjoy their home life. The project will have open areas with many use facilities (playground areas and open spaces) designed to make your life experience more enjoyable. The buildings will have different areas of apartments-underground parking.
